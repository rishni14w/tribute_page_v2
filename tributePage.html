EXO

Timeline of EXO

  • 2011 - Forms South Korean-Chinese boy band consisting of eight South Korean members and four Chinese members.
  • 2012 - Debuts as EXO-K and EXO-M with the single 'Mama' in South Korea and China. Thereafter receives 'Best New Asian Group' award and the 'Newcomer' award.
  • 2013 - Releases the 1st studio album name 'XOXO' in Korean and Mandarin. Becomes the 1st South Korean artists to sell over one million copies in 12 years. Participates in their 1st reality show. Wins Song of the Year at Melon Music Awards, Disc Daesang at Golden Disc Awards, Album of the year at Mnet Asian Music Awards, Daesang at Seoul Music Awards and Most Popular Group Award at Top Chinese Music Awards.
  • 2014 - Releases 'Overdose' and becomes the artists with the most pre-ordered Korean Extended Play in history. Becomes the highest-ever charting male Korean group. Releases 1st live album and becomes Japan's best-selling K-pop artist of the year. Performs their 1st concert tour and breaks the record for quickest concert sell-out by a Korean artist. Kris and Luhan leave the group.
  • 2015 - Performs 2nd world tour. Releases second studio album 'Exodus'. Sets a new pre-order record and sells over a million copies for the second time. Wins Album of the year at Mnet Asian Music Awards. Becomes the charting Korean Artist in US Billboard 200 and 1st K-pop group 2nd Korean artist to enter Billboard's Canadian Hot 100. Tao leaves the group. Holds a dome concert in South Korea and becomes the 1st to do so. Releases Japanese debut single and becomes the all-time best-selling debut single by a Korean artist in Japan. Releases 'Sing for You' Extended Play and becomes the highest first week sales by a Korean artist. Obtains the highest rank held by a Korean artist by being ranked number seven in top ten pop culture leaders.
  • 2016 - Releases 3rd studio album "Ex'Act" and breaks their own record for highest first week sales of a Korean album. This album also sells over 1.17 million copies which makes EXO 'triple million seller'. Performs third concert tour. First sub unit of EXO debuts as EXO-CBX. Lay debuts a solo artist.
  • 2017 - Releases fourth studio album 'The War' and sets new record for pre-orders and highest first-week sales. Becomes the artists to win the most Daesang awards at Mnet Asian Music Awards as per Guinness World Records. Obtains the 100th win in Music shows. Performs the third world tour and also sells over 1.6 million copies of the fourth album and earns the 'Quadruple million seller' title.
  • 2018 - The Dubai Fountain at Burj Khalifa Lake, Dubai plays 'Power' which is one of EXO's song and becomes the first K-pop song to be played. Releases first Japanese album which is certified gold by Recording Industry Association of Japan. Sings national anthem at the opening ceremony and performs at the closing ceremony of 2018 Winter Olympics. Receives official commemorative medals for spreading Korean culture globally. Releases sixth album "Don't Mess Up My Tempo" and exceed their pre-record record. Also earns the title 'Quintuple million sellers' and becomes the 1st artists to exceed ten million total album sales in South Korea.
  • 2019 - Chen and Baekhyun debuts as a soloist. Xiumin and D.O enlists for military service. Remaining members practices for upcoming concert tour as well as upcoming second sub unit debut by Chanyeol and Sehun.
